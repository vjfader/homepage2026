VFX Music Visualization

I create realtime visuals that move with sound, space, and performance energy.

I am a Berlin-based motion designer, VFX artist, and creative technologist with 10+ years of experience creating visuals across live events, immersive platforms, branded content, and digital products. My VFX work brings together audio-reactive systems, particle-driven motion, realtime rendering, and visual storytelling designed for music-led experiences.

Unreal Engine I build Niagara-driven visual systems for immersive music and VR environments.
Realtime I focus on visuals that react live instead of feeling like fixed playback content.
Immersive My work supports concerts, virtual worlds, product experiences, and performance-first projects.

How I approach music visualization

I create realtime visual systems and motion-driven experiences for music, immersive platforms, and digital products. My work combines particle systems, environment animation, motion design, and interactive visual storytelling across Unreal Engine, Unity, and After Effects. From audio-reactive visuals and VR entertainment to product-facing motion assets, I focus on building visuals that feel responsive, polished, and deeply connected to the experience.

I am especially interested in the point where technical systems and visual craft meet: particles that react to rhythm, environmental motion that supports atmosphere, and generative behaviors that make a performance feel alive. Every project starts with the same question for me: how can the visuals feel native to the music rather than layered on top of it?
